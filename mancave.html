Our Story

London Craft,
Larnaca Home

BAM'S BARBERS has been a cornerstone of Larnaca's grooming scene since 2015. Behind every cut is over 25 years of hands-on experience from West End London — one of the world's most demanding barbering environments.

Our shop is a proper mancave — superhero gear, movie posters, the works — and everyone's welcome regardless of age or hair type. No rush. No shortcuts. You walk out looking your best.
